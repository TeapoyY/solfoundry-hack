### 分析策略更新
- **多桶概率**: P(final in [lo,hi]) = P(remaining in [lo-confirmed, hi-confirmed])
- **Bucket Combos**: 相邻桶组合，EV = sum(P_i) - sum(price_i)
- **小时倒计时**: hours_remaining + tweets_per_hour_needed + in_peak_window
- **高峰分析**: Elon's Peak Hours UTC: 22,23,0,1,7,8 (late night US + morning US)
- **二元概率**: P(YES) = sum of buckets where lo >= target
